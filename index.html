Our Story

Coffee made with intention, served without ceremony.

Mzuri Coffee started with a simple idea: that a really good cup shouldn't come with an attitude. We source thoughtfully, dial in every shot by hand, and keep the room warm enough that you forget to check the time.

Whether you're in for a flat white before work or settling in with a slice of cake and a friend, this is a place built around the pause — not the rush.

Come In For

Three good reasons to pull up a chair.

Specialty Coffee

Flat whites, Americanos and pour-overs made on a machine we actually fuss over — every cup pulled to order, never rushed.

House Pastries

Butter croissants, chocolate cake and daily bakes from our own kitchen. Simple, done properly, and usually still warm.

A Table to Linger At

No rush, no pressure to order again in ten minutes. Bring a book, a laptop, or just good company.

From our tables to yours.

A few real moments from the counter — croissants still warm, iced matcha in the afternoon sun, and the everyday ritual of a good cup.

The Craft

Every cup gets the same attention — first or last order of the day.

Good coffee is mostly about not cutting corners. That means fresh-ground beans, a dialled-in grind, and a barista who tastes before it goes out — not just on a good day, but every day.

It's a small thing. But it's the difference you can actually taste in the cup.
